A case study in strategic orchestration

instead of agency model

Creative team, technical team, production team, project management. Coordination layers, approval chains, diluted vision.

we questioned
  • Whether coordination layers serve premium brand outcomes
  • What one strategic director + AI capabilities could achieve
  • How to maintain unified vision while building sophisticated infrastructure

The inherited structure

  • E-commerce platforms optimize for volume, not intention
  • Standard Shopify themes prioritize conversion metrics over brand cohesion
  • Agency models add coordination costs that dilute creative control
  • Fashion brands forced to compromise aesthetic for platform limitations
  • Template-driven approaches conflict with scarcity-driven positioning

What wasn't serving the outcome

  • Generic checkout experiences break brand immersion
  • Coordination across teams dilutes singular creative vision
  • Platform defaults prioritize merchant efficiency over customer experience
  • Standard approaches force premium brands into commodity patterns

What we built instead

  • Custom headless architecture (GitHub Pages + Shopify backend)
  • Vanilla JavaScript maintaining lightweight, intentional experience
  • Brand voice system spanning product copy to production specifications
  • Complete tech pack documentation with Pantone accuracy
  • International expansion strategy preserving margin structure
  • Cart synchronization maintaining brand cohesion through checkout
