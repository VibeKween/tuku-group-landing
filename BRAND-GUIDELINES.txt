# TUKU GROUP Brand Guidelines

## Core Philosophy

**"Craft over noise. Fewer, better, slower."**

- **Independent by choice** - Privately held creative house
- **Culture as compass** - Signals for the present tense
- **Intentional craft** - Working with precision and depth
- **Meaningful ventures** - For brands ready to refine their vision

## Brand Identity

### Company Name
- **Legal**: Tuku Group, LLC
- **Brand**: TUKU GROUP (all caps)
- **Founded**: 2025
- **Structure**: Independent and privately held

### Tagline & Positioning
- **Primary**: "Independent Creative House"
- **Mission**: "Independent ideas. Thoughtful brands. Assembled with intention."
- **Purpose**: "A creative house for ventures that hold meaning."

## Visual Identity

### Typography
- **Primary Font**: JetBrains Mono (all weights: 300, 400, 500, 600)
- **Fallback**: Courier New, monospace
- **Style**: Monospace aesthetic throughout all brand touchpoints

### Color Palette

**Core Colors:**
--color-black: #000000    /* Primary text and backgrounds */
--color-white: #FFFFFF    /* Background and inverted text */
--color-gold: #C19A4B     /* Philosophy headers and accents */

**Extended Palette (Payment/TUKU Blue):**
--color-blue-light: #f0f9ff    /* Light backgrounds */
--color-blue-medium: #e0f2fe   /* Medium selections */
--color-blue-accent: #5691c8   /* Links and buttons */
--color-blue-deep: #4a7ba7     /* Hover states */

### Logo & Favicon
- **Favicon**: Simple cloud icon in light blue (#5691c8)
- **Logo**: Text-based "TUKU GROUP" in JetBrains Mono
- **Social Image**: Custom OG image at 1200x630px

## Design Principles

### Layout Standards
- **Max Width**: 600px centered content container
- **Vertical Spacing**: 80px between content blocks (60px mobile)
- **Section Structure**: Sequential <section class="block"> elements
- **Whitespace**: Massive intentional spacing emphasizes minimal aesthetic

### Content Philosophy
- **Manifesto-driven**: Each section communicates single concept
- **Declarative tone**: Direct statements without explanation  
- **Intentional brevity**: Every word serves purpose
- **Professional voice**: Without corporate jargon

### Typography Hierarchy
h1: Main brand name (TUKU GROUP)
h2: Section headers (visually hidden for accessibility)
.philosophy-header: Gold accent spans for key concepts
p: Body text with 1.8 line height

## Brand Voice & Messaging

### Key Messages
- **"We bring our beliefs to form"** - Action-oriented philosophy
- **"Selective collaborations"** - Quality over quantity
- **"Clarity first"** - Process starts with listening
- **"Considered execution"** - Balance creativity with purpose

### Content Patterns
- **Philosophy Headers**: Key concepts in gold .philosophy-header spans
- **Line Breaks**: Strategic <br> for emphasis and rhythm
- **Contact Approach**: "Start a conversation" (not "Get a quote")

## Digital Standards

### Website Architecture
- **Homepage**: Manifesto-style single page
- **Approach**: Methodology and philosophy
- **Studio/Payment**: Private portal for qualified leads only
- **Cloud Background**: Subtle animated elements

### SEO & Meta Data
- **Structured Data**: Organization schema with full business details
- **Social Media**: Optimized Open Graph and Twitter Card meta tags
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics 4 (G-5KTM9YBETS)

### Technical Requirements
- **Mobile-first**: Single breakpoint at 768px
- **Performance**: Sub-2 second load times
- **Accessibility**: Semantic HTML with screen reader support
- **Pure Code**: No frameworks, libraries, or build tools

## Usage Guidelines

### Do's
- ✅ Maintain ultra-minimal aesthetic aligned with "craft over noise"
- ✅ Use JetBrains Mono consistently across all touchpoints
- ✅ Emphasize whitespace and intentional spacing
- ✅ Keep messaging concise and declarative
- ✅ Lead with philosophy before process

### Don'ts
- ❌ Add unnecessary visual elements or decorations
- ❌ Use multiple fonts or mix typography systems
- ❌ Create cluttered layouts or dense information blocks
- ❌ Use corporate jargon or sales-heavy language
- ❌ Display pricing publicly (private portal only)

This brand system supports TUKU's core philosophy of intentional craft while maintaining professional credibility and visual consistency across all touchpoints.
